# Step 4A: ROI and Bay Proportion

## Purpose

This sub-stage defines the working region for 2D geometry analysis and establishes the bay proportion evidence used by the later Step 4 stages.

## What you do here

- position the ROI over the relevant vault area
- adjust the ROI so it isolates the bay to be analysed
- review the evidence layers inside the ROI
- save the ROI before continuing to the next Step 4 sub-stage

## Why it matters

The later reference-point, typology, and reconstruction stages depend on a stable and correctly positioned ROI. If the ROI is poorly defined, the rest of Step 4 becomes unreliable.

## Expected result

Before continuing within Step 4, you should have:

- a saved ROI
- a clearly defined analysis area
- a bay proportion setup that is ready for the next sub-stage
