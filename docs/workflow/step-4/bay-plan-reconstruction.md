# Step 4D: Bay-Plan Reconstruction

## Purpose

This final Step 4 sub-stage reconstructs the bay plan from the prepared ROI, reference points, and matching results.

## What you do here

- review the available matching output
- run the reconstruction process
- inspect the reconstructed bay-plan geometry
- confirm the result before continuing to 3D reprojection

## Why it matters

This is the final 2D interpretation step before the workflow returns to 3D. The reconstruction created here becomes one of the most important outputs for the next stage.

## Expected result

Before leaving Step 4, you should have:

- a completed reconstruction result
- a bay-plan interpretation that is ready to be reprojected into 3D
