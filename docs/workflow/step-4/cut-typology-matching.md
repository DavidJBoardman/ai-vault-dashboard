# Step 4C: Cut-Typology Matching

## Purpose

This sub-stage compares the prepared geometry against the available cut-typology patterns.

## What you do here

- review the prepared ROI and reference points
- run the cut-typology matching process
- inspect the matching result and evidence
- confirm that the selected result is credible before reconstruction

## Why it matters

This stage connects the prepared geometric evidence to a specific typological interpretation. That interpretation becomes an important input to the reconstruction stage.

## Expected result

Before continuing within Step 4, you should have:

- a completed matching result
- enough confidence in the selected typology to proceed into reconstruction
